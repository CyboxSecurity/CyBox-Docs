# Invite your team

CyBox works best when security and engineering can look at the same findings and act from the same workflow.

## Invite a teammate

1. Open **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the teammate's email address
4. Choose a role
5. Send the invite

## Recommended roles

| Role | Best for |
|------|----------|
| **Owner** | Organization setup, billing, integrations, and full control |
| **Admin** | Day-to-day management of assets, scans, and members |
| **Member** | Engineers and security users who review findings and use AutoFix |
| **Viewer** | Stakeholders who need read-only visibility |

## Rollout tip

A simple first setup is:

- 1 Owner
- 1 to 2 Admins
- Engineers as Members
- Leadership as Viewers

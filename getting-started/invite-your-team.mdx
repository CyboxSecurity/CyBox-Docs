# Invite your team

CyBox works best when security and engineering can look at the same findings and act from the same workflow.

## Invite a teammate

1. Open **Organization Settings → Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the teammate's email address
4. Choose a role
5. Send the invite

The teammate receives an email with a link to join. Once they accept, they land directly in your workspace.

## Roles

CyBox has three roles: **Owner**, **Admin**, and **Member**.

| Action | Member | Admin | Owner |
|--------|--------|-------|-------|
| View dashboards, findings, and assets | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Use AutoFix and CyBot AI | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Invite team members | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Change scan frequency | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Rename the workspace | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Remove members | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Delete the workspace | — | — | ✓ |

### Owner

Full control over the workspace. The person who sets up the organization is automatically the Owner. There can only be one Owner — to transfer ownership, the current Owner must assign the role to another member first.

### Admin

Can manage the team and configure the workspace — inviting members, changing scan frequency, and renaming the organization. Cannot delete the workspace or transfer ownership.

### Member

Can view all security data, use AutoFix, and interact with CyBot AI. Cannot change any organization settings or invite others. The right role for engineers and security reviewers.

## Rollout tip

A simple first setup:

- 1 Owner — the security lead or team manager
- 1 to 2 Admins — engineers who need to manage settings
- Everyone else as Members

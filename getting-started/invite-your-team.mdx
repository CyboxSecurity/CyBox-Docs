title: "Invite Your Team"
description: "Add teammates to your CyBox organization so they can view findings, trigger scans, and collaborate on remediation."
---

## How to Invite Members

1. Go to **Settings → Team** in the CyBox dashboard
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the teammate's email address
4. Select their role (see below)
5. Click **Send Invite**

The invitee will receive an email with a link to join your CyBox organization.

---

## Roles

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | Full access — billing, settings, integrations, all assets |
| **Admin** | Manage assets, scans, team members. No billing access |
| **Member** | View findings, trigger scans, use AutoFix |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to findings and dashboard |

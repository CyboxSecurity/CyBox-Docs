## What CyBox does

CyBox is the control layer between AI-assisted development and production.

It is built for teams where AI tools are writing code, deploying apps, and moving faster than traditional security review can keep up with.

### The control gap

AI now writes, edits, and helps ship software alongside developers — producing code, applications, and external assets faster than most security programs were built to govern.

The result is a new kind of risk: code no one fully reviewed, services no one approved, and exposure no one can clearly see. CyBox closes that gap before it becomes a production problem.

### How CyBox works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover">
    Connect your repositories and deployed web assets. CyBox builds a continuous inventory of what your organization has — code, dependencies, secrets, infrastructure definitions, and live internet-facing surfaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate">
    CyBox scans connected assets and tests live web surfaces to understand what is actually exposed, what matters most, and where real business risk is building.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Control">
    Review, warn on, and block risky changes at the pull request level before they ship. Use guided remediation and AutoFix to move from finding to resolution without creating more manual work.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who CyBox is for

- **Security teams** that need a control point over AI-driven development without slowing engineering down
- **Engineering leaders** who need to ship fast and stay accountable for what gets deployed
- **Organizations** adopting AI development tools that want visibility before exposure becomes a problem

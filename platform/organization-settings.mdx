## Overview

Organization settings control how your CyBox workspace is configured. Go to **Organization settings** in the sidebar to manage these options.

Settings are organized across four tabs: **General**, **Members**, **Scanning**, and **Danger zone**.

---

## General

Configure the basics of your organization.

| Setting | What it controls |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Organization logo** | The logo shown in your CyBox workspace |
| **Organization name** | The display name for your organization |

Changes are saved individually using the **Save** button next to each field.

---

## Members

Manage who has access to your CyBox organization.

### Inviting a member

1. Enter the teammate's email address
2. Select a role
3. Click **Invite**

The invitee receives an email with a link to join the organization.

### Roles

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | Full access — billing, settings, integrations, all assets |
| **Admin** | Manage assets, scans, and team members. No billing access |
| **Member** | Review findings, trigger scans, use AutoFix |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to findings and the dashboard |

### Managing existing members

Active members appear in the **Active members** tab. Pending invites that have not yet been accepted appear in **Pending invitations**.

---

## Scanning

Control how often CyBox automatically scans your active assets.

| Frequency | Schedule |
|-----------|----------|
| **Daily** | Every 24 hours |
| **Every 3 days** | Every 72 hours |
| **Weekly** | Every 7 days |

The **Next Scheduled Scan** section shows the upcoming scan time for each active asset. Assets without an active schedule show as **Not scheduled**.

<Note>
Scan frequency applies to scheduled background scans. PR Firewall scans and manual scans are not affected by this setting — they run immediately when triggered.
</Note>

---

## Danger zone

The Danger zone contains destructive actions that cannot be undone. These actions require explicit confirmation before proceeding.

<Warning>
Actions in the Danger zone are permanent. Review carefully before proceeding.
</Warning>

## Overview

Account settings control your personal profile within CyBox. These settings are specific to your individual account — not shared across your organization. Go to **Account settings** in the sidebar to manage them.

Settings are organized across three tabs: **General**, **Security**, and **Danger zone**.

---

## General

| Setting | What it controls |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Avatar** | Your profile picture — click the image to upload a new one |
| **Your name** | The display name shown to teammates |
| **Your email** | The email address used for login and notifications. Changes require email confirmation before becoming active |

Each field saves independently using the **Save** button next to it.

---

## Security

Manage the security of your personal account, including password changes and active sessions.

---

## Danger zone

The Danger zone contains destructive account-level actions that cannot be undone.

<Warning>
Actions in the Danger zone are permanent. Review carefully before proceeding.
</Warning>

# Run your first scan

## Why the first scan matters

Your first scan creates the baseline.

That helps your team separate:

- Existing backlog
- Newly introduced risk
- Assets that need the most attention first

## Run the scan

1. Open the asset in CyBox
2. Click **Scan Now**
3. Wait for the scan to complete
4. Open **Findings** and sort by severity

## What to do after the scan

- Review Critical and High findings first
- Identify issues that should be fixed before rollout expands
- Decide when to turn on PR Firewall for that repository

# Review findings

A clean review flow keeps CyBox useful.

## Recommended triage order

1. Critical
2. High
3. Introduced findings in active pull requests
4. Medium findings in important assets

## What to look at inside a finding

- Severity
- Affected asset
- Evidence
- Why it matters
- What to do next
- AutoFix availability

## Possible outcomes

- Fix manually
- Generate an AutoFix pull request
- Ignore with reason
- Re-scan after remediation

<Tip>
Treat Informational findings as context, not urgency. They are useful, but they should not dominate the workflow.
</Tip>

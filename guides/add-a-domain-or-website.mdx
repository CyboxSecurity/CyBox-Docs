---

## When to Use This

Add a domain or URL when you want CyBox to:
- Actively test a live web app for exploitable vulnerabilities
- Monitor a deployed environment (staging, production, preview)
- Track posture checks for assets discovered via Shadow Apps

---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Assets">
    In the CyBox dashboard, click **Assets** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Asset">
    Click **Add Asset → Website / URL**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the URL">
    Enter the full URL including protocol — e.g. `https://app.yourcompany.com`. The asset must be publicly accessible for DAST to run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. A DAST scan starts automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Scan Results

Once the scan completes (typically 2–5 minutes):
- Go to **Findings** and filter by this asset
- Or open the asset from **Assets** to see findings scoped to it

---

## Auto-Discovered Assets

If you've connected Vercel or Render, CyBox automatically discovers and adds deployments as URL assets. You can trigger DAST on any of them from the asset detail page.

---

## Important Notes

<Warning>
Only add URLs for assets you own or have explicit permission to test. DAST sends active HTTP requests to the target.
</Warning>

<Note>
CyBox does not crawl beyond the root URL. Scans target the specified URL and known subpaths — not a full site crawl.
</Note>

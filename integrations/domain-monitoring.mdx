## Overview

CyBox connects to your deployment platforms to automatically discover apps, staging environments, and preview deployments — including ones your security team may not know about (Shadow Apps).

Once discovered, assets are added to your CyBox inventory and scanned with DAST on a regular schedule.

## Connect Vercel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In the CyBox dashboard, go to **Integrations → Domain Monitoring**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Vercel">
    Click the **Connect Vercel** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize CyBox">
    You'll be redirected to Vercel. Authorize CyBox to access your Vercel account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discovery Begins">
    CyBox immediately syncs your Vercel deployments and adds them as URL assets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Connect Render

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Render API Key">
    In Render, go to **Account Settings → API Keys** and generate a new key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In the CyBox dashboard, go to **Integrations → Domain Monitoring**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Render">
    Enter your Render API key and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discovery Begins">
    CyBox syncs your Render services and adds them as URL assets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Sync Schedule

CyBox syncs with connected platforms **every 6 hours**. New deployments are discovered and added automatically. Removed deployments are marked **Inactive** — their findings are retained for audit purposes.

---

## What Gets Scanned

For each discovered URL asset, CyBox runs:
- **5 posture checks** (security headers, HTTPS, exposed paths, etc.)
- **Full DAST scan** on the initial discovery and every 24 hours after

See [Live Exposure Validation](/features/live-exposure-validation) for details on what DAST checks.

---

## Disconnecting a Platform

To remove a platform integration:

1. Go to **Integrations → Domain Monitoring**
2. Click **Disconnect** next to the platform
3. Existing assets and findings are retained — only future syncs stop

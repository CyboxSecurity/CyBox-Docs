## How to connect an integration

1. Go to **Integrations** in the CyBox sidebar
2. Find the integration you want to connect
3. Click the card — each integration has its own setup flow
4. Follow the authorization steps
5. CyBox will immediately begin syncing data from the connected service

<Note>
Integrations marked **Available soon** are on the roadmap. To request early access or vote on priority, contact [support@cybox.ai](mailto:support@cybox.ai).
</Note>

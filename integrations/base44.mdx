## Overview

Base44 is an AI-powered app builder that generates and deploys web applications from natural language. CyBox connects to Base44 to automatically discover every deployed app, scan it for live exposure issues, and keep it visible alongside your other assets.

---

## What CyBox monitors

Once connected, CyBox continuously tracks every app deployed through your Base44 account:

- **Live exposure validation** — active scanning for exposed sensitive paths, missing security headers, open redirects, and information disclosure
- **Posture checks** — HTTPS enforcement, cookie flags, CORS misconfigurations
- **Asset inventory** — every Base44 deployment visible in one place alongside your repositories and other cloud assets
- **Shadow app detection** — apps spun up by teammates that may have bypassed a formal security review

---

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In the CyBox dashboard, go to **Integrations** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Base44">
    Locate the **Base44** card under the **External Exposure** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click the Base44 card and follow the authorization flow to connect your Base44 account to CyBox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discovery begins">
    CyBox immediately syncs your Base44 deployments and adds them as URL assets. A posture scan runs automatically on each discovered app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What happens after connecting

- All active Base44 deployments appear under **Assets** with source tagged as **Base44**
- Each asset is scanned automatically and findings appear in **Findings**
- CyBox syncs every 6 hours — new deployments are discovered and added automatically
- Removed deployments are marked **Inactive** — their findings are retained for audit purposes

---

## Suggested rollout

Connect Base44 early — especially if your team uses it to build internal tools or customer-facing apps. AI-generated apps can expose APIs, admin paths, or sensitive configs that traditional scanners miss.

<Note>
Base44 apps are scanned whether or not they have a custom domain. CyBox discovers and scans Base44-generated URLs automatically.
</Note>

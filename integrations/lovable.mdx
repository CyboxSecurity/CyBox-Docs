## Overview

Lovable is an AI-powered app builder that lets teams ship full-stack web apps from a prompt. CyBox connects to Lovable to automatically discover every deployed app, scan it for live exposure issues, and keep it visible alongside your other assets.

---

## What CyBox monitors

Once connected, CyBox continuously tracks every app deployed through your Lovable account:

- **Live exposure validation** — active scanning for exposed sensitive paths, missing security headers, open redirects, and information disclosure
- **Posture checks** — HTTPS enforcement, cookie flags, CORS misconfigurations
- **Asset inventory** — every Lovable deployment visible in one place alongside your repositories and other cloud assets
- **Shadow app detection** — apps spun up by teammates that may have bypassed a formal security review

---

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In the CyBox dashboard, go to **Integrations** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Lovable">
    Locate the **Lovable** card under the **External Exposure** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click the Lovable card and follow the authorization flow to connect your Lovable account to CyBox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discovery begins">
    CyBox immediately syncs your Lovable deployments and adds them as URL assets. A posture scan runs automatically on each discovered app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What happens after connecting

- All active Lovable deployments appear under **Assets** with source tagged as **Lovable**
- Each asset is scanned automatically and findings appear in **Findings**
- CyBox syncs every 6 hours — new deployments are discovered and added automatically
- Removed deployments are marked **Inactive** — their findings are retained for audit purposes

---

## Suggested rollout

Connect Lovable as soon as your team starts using it for customer-facing or production apps. Even internal tools and staging apps built with Lovable can expose sensitive data if misconfigured.

<Note>
If a Lovable app has no custom domain yet and uses a Lovable-generated URL, CyBox will still discover and scan it. You don't need a custom domain to get coverage.
</Note>
